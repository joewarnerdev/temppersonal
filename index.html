My Story


I have had a passion for web design as long as I have had a computer. I started off on Geocities (yes, I know this dates me!) and once I realized the features were too limited, I found a better way. As a teenager, I taught myself HTML and Flash (RIP), downloaded a FTP program, and anyone who needed a website for anything from my dad's baking business, a card game hobby, or a local punk band, I was eager to help. I started attending college at UW Waukesha and Youngstown State, but was unable to finish. After some time in other fields, I decided I was going to master what I loved so much and started web development again.
